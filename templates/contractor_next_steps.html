Thank you for registering. Here are the next crucial steps for your visit:

Safety File Approval

Prior to work commencement, please ensure you have handed in your **safety file for approval**. This file must include:

  • Risk Assessments
  • Qualifications
  • Identification
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) requirements and issue documentation

Your safety file must be approved before any work begins on site.

Mandatory Induction

Upon approval of your safety file, you will undergo a **mandatory safety induction** prior to work commencement. This ensures you are fully aware of site-specific safety protocols.

On-site Safety Checks

Please be aware that during your time on site, random checks will be conducted to ensure adherence to safety protocols and the consistent use of **required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)**.

Access control and initial non-disclosure form signing are handled at reception.
